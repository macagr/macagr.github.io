## About RandomByte

I always like comparing the learning process to a journey: we may or may not
know our goal, but getting there is full of unexpected surprises and new
things to learn. This blog is where I compile thoughts, learnings, and
different perspectives developed through my journey into information security.

## About me

I'm a pentester and cybersecurity consultant focused on the offensive side of
security — cloud, containers, and the application layer. I enjoy learning and
writing, which is one of the reasons I started this blog. It serves both as a
test of my own understanding and as a place to polish my writing.

## What you'll find here

- Cloud attack/defense research (AWS, Azure, GCP)
- Container and Kubernetes security
- Notes from certifications and training
- Policy-as-code and governance tooling
